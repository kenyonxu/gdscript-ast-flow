AST 节点层次（与 Godot 4.7 对齐）

ASTNode (基类: line, column) │ ├── ClassNode extends_id, classname_id, is_tool, annotations[], members[] │ ├─ members[] = FunctionNode | VariableNode | SignalNode | EnumNode | ClassNode | AnnotationNode │ ├── FunctionNode name, params[], return_type, body: SuiteNode, is_static, is_coroutine ├── VariableNode name, datatype, initializer, setter, getter, is_onready, is_export ├── SignalNode name, params[] ├── EnumNode name, values[{name, value}] │ ├── SuiteNode statements[] — 语句块容器（函数体/if体/循环体） │ ├── StatementNode (语句基类) │ ├── IfNode condition, true_branch: SuiteNode, false_branch │ ├── WhileNode condition, body: SuiteNode │ ├── ForNode var_name, iterable, body: SuiteNode │ ├── MatchNode test_expr, branches[{patterns[], guard, body}] │ ├── ReturnNode value │ ├── BreakNode / ContinueNode / PassNode │ ├── AssertNode condition, message │ ├── AwaitNode expression │ └── ExpressionStatementNode wraps expression as statement │ ├── ExpressionNode (表达式基类) │ ├── BinaryOpNode op, left, right │ ├── UnaryOpNode op, operand │ ├── TernaryOpNode condition, true_expr, false_expr │ ├── CallNode callee, arguments[] │ ├── SubscriptNode base, index (a[b]) │ ├── AttributeNode base, name (a.b) │ ├── CastNode expression, type (x as Type) │ ├── TypeTestNode expression, type (x is Type) │ ├── AssignmentNode target, value, op (=, +=, -=...) │ ├── LambdaNode params[], body │ ├── ArrayNode elements[] │ ├── DictionaryNode pairs[{key, value}] │ └── 叶子节点: │ IdentifierNode name │ LiteralNode value (Variant) │ SelfNode / SuperNode / PreloadNode │ ├── AnnotationNode name, arguments[] (@export, @onready, @tool...) └── TypeNode type_name, container_element_types[] (如 Array[int])

3.4 旧 AST → 4.7 新 AST 节点映射

3.4 (当前工程)→4.7 (目标)变化
TreeClass→ClassNode+annotations, +members 含 AnnotationNode
TreeFunction→FunctionNode+is_coroutine, +return_type 改用 TypeNode
TreeVar / TreeConst→VariableNode+setter/getter 内联, +is_onready/is_export
TreeIf→IfNode结构类似
TreeMatch / TreeCase→MatchNode + MatchBranchNode+when 语法, +guard 表达式, +pattern
TreeOnReady→AnnotationNode + VariableNode结构完全改变
TreeSignal→SignalNode类似
TreeEnum→EnumNode类似
ExprBinOp / ExprUnOp→BinaryOpNode / UnaryOpNode结构类似
ExprCallFunc→CallNode+callable 调用模式
ExprSubscription→SubscriptNode + AttributeNode拆分 a[b] 和 a.b
ExprIf→TernaryOpNode结构类似
(新增)+LambdaNode, CastNode, TypeTestNode, AwaitNode4.7 新特性
